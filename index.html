Since I was a little child, I have been fascinated and influenced by many different artworks. The talented artists and their magnificent masterpieces have filled me with admiration. I have always been passionate about drawing, expressing my ideas on paper, and imitating masterpieces. Along the way from my elementary school to high school, I have considered drawing as my hobby and taking many art classes in achieving a high standard of artistic personal character.

Under the guidance of my father, I needed to practice drawing the same object repeatedly until it was acceptable. Eventually I could add up several things and even scenery. The basic artistic skill was slowly developed by this repeating and boring method. For school artwork assignments, my father would give me guidance and advice on how to apply different types of art materials. As my artistic skills arose and were recognized by my grade school teachers, I was nominated to create a cultural drawing for celebrating the opening of Walmart in Chinatown, Los Angeles. I also got second place for drawing the 2014-2015 school yearbook cover.
